The Gentleman in Moscow

The Gentleman in Moscow immerses us in another elegrant drawn era with the story of Count Alexander Rostov. In 1922, he is deemed an unrepentant aristocrat by a Bolshevik tribunal, the count is sentenced to house arrest in the Metropol. The Metropol is a grand hotel in Moscow across the street from the Kremlin.

The book beautifully explores themes of resilience, human connection, and adapting to change.

Author Amor Towles was the author of the book

The ending of the book is controversial and many people think differently of the ending. At the end, the Count began making plans to escape from the hotel together with Sofia. They were successful. The Count went to his family's estate and met Anna at the tavern waiting for him.
